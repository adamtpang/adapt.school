A Pragmatic Approach to Child Sovereignty

Our Philosophy

At Adapt.School, we believe in the inherent capability of children to direct their own learning journey. We provide a space where children's autonomy and natural curiosity are respected and nurtured.

Our Environment

Set in the green spaces of Forest City, our school integrates with nature to create a living classroom where sustainable practices and ecological awareness are part of everyday learning.

Our Approach

We combine freedom with structure, allowing children to develop at their own pace while ensuring they build essential skills for a changing world. Here, children become architects of their own education.

For Parents

Join a community where your child's unique needs and interests shape their education. At Adapt.School, we partner with parents to create personalized learning experiences that honor each child's natural development path.

Our approach helps children develop:

  • Self-direction and intrinsic motivation
  • Critical thinking and problem-solving skills
  • Emotional intelligence and social awareness
  • Environmental stewardship and sustainable living practices

We maintain small class sizes and regular communication with families to ensure a truly collaborative approach to your child's growth.

For Teachers & Staff

Are you passionate about alternative education and child-led learning? Join our team of dedicated educators who are reimagining what school can be.

We're looking for individuals who:

  • Believe in children's capacity to direct their own learning
  • Value open communication and collaborative approaches
  • Bring creativity and flexibility to the learning environment
  • Are committed to their own growth and learning

At Adapt.School, teachers are guides and mentors, creating rich environments for discovery rather than delivering standardized curricula.
